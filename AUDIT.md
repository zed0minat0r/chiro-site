## Observations on Re-Review

A few notes from this fresh pass, none of which change the score:

1. **Copy quality remains strong.** Re-reading the hero, services, journey, and FAQ sections with fresh eyes — the writing is genuinely patient-centred and avoids clinical jargon. This is a real asset that most competitor sites lack.

2. **Animation compliance: still clean.** No new animations have crept in. Scroll-reveal fade-in and FAQ accordion only. No glows, shimmers, bounces, or lifts. AGENT-RULES fully respected.

3. **Mobile experience holds up.** Nav burger, sticky CTA bar, stacked layouts, 48px tap targets — all still solid. No regressions.

4. **The placeholder silhouette in About is still the most damaging single element.** Every time I look at this section I score it lower in my head. A real patient sees an empty frame where a doctor's face should be and immediately questions legitimacy.

5. **Form still non-functional.** `action="https://formspree.io/f/placeholder"` — this remains a hard blocker. A patient who fills out the form and hits submit gets nothing.
