3 Quick Wins for Your Back & Neck

Small daily habits make a bigger difference than most people realize. These three tips cost nothing and take less than a minute each.

  • Feet flat on the floor Sit with both feet flat — no crossed legs, no feet tucked under. It takes the stress off your lower spine and keeps your hips level throughout the day.
  • Sleep on your side with a pillow between your knees This keeps your spine in neutral alignment overnight and reduces strain on your hips and lower back — especially important if you wake up stiff in the mornings.
  • Take a 5-minute walk every hour Sitting compresses spinal discs and tightens your hip flexors fast. Standing up and moving every hour is one of the single best things you can do for your long-term spine health.

Common Questions

What Patients Ask

We want you to feel confident before you even walk through the door.

Will the adjustment hurt?

Most patients feel immediate relief after an adjustment. You may experience minor soreness in the treated area for 24–48 hours afterward — similar to how muscles feel after a workout. We use gentle techniques and always explain exactly what to expect before we begin.

How many visits will I need?

Every case is different. Many patients feel significant improvement within 3–6 visits for acute issues. Chronic conditions may take longer. After your first visit, Dr. Jason will give you an honest timeline — no open-ended treatment plans, no pressure to commit upfront.

Is chiropractic care covered by insurance?

Most major insurance plans include chiropractic coverage. We accept most insurance and will verify your benefits before your first visit so there are no surprises. We also offer affordable self-pay rates for those without coverage.

What happens during my first visit?

Your first visit starts with a thorough consultation — Dr. Jason will listen to your concerns, review your history, and perform an examination. If treatment is appropriate that day, he'll explain his findings clearly and discuss your options before proceeding. No surprise procedures.

Do I need a referral from my doctor?

No referral is needed in Pennsylvania. You can book directly. If you're already working with another healthcare provider, we're happy to coordinate care and communicate with your physician.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We offer same-day appointments when available — call us in the morning for the best chance of a same-day slot. New patients are always welcome. Fill out the form below or call and we'll get you in as quickly as possible.
